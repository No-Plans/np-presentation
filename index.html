Team

Core Capabilities

Educated in Switzerland, we are now located in London and New York. We have a background in graphic design and typography with ten years of experience in digital design and development; we work with leaders in creative industries, launching e-commerce solutions and consulting on new brands in need of their first online presence.

A team of three people (covering design, development, project management) will be assigned to your project, with the option of bringing in experts from our network.

Network

We regularly collaborate with specialists in type design and branding and with professionals in research, publishing, strategy and technical development.

Typical Process

1 — Research

First we outline the structure and goals for the brand’s online presence in conversation with the client. Then we create a blueprint which includes a list of brand priorities, analysis of industry references and choice of technology.

2 — Design

Starting with the blueprint, visual explorations and brand understanding, we conceive different design directions. We prototype mobile experience and transitions for better sensing.

3 — Development

We structure the development phase to allow time for testing and content population. Then websites are migrated to the server to be tested with all screen sizes, devices and main browsers before launch.
