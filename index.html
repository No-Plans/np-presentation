Team

Team

Started in Switzerland, then moved to London and New York, we have a background in graphic design and typography with 10 years of experience in digital design and development: we have worked with clients in the creative field, conceived e-commerce solutions and consulted for new ventures in need of an online presence.

A team of 3 people (covering design, developement, strategy/project management) will be assigned to your project, with the option of bringing in experts from our network.

Network

We work on a regular basis with designers specialized in typography and branding (ATMA, Robert Huber) and collaborate with professionals from the Are.na community in fields such as research, publishing, strategy and techincal development.

Typical Process

1 — Research

We outline the structure and goals of the online presence: conversation with the client to set a list of priorities, research of industry references and choice of technology.

2 — Design

Starting with visual explorations and the client’s feedback, we design a few directions towards the brief goals with the brand in mind. The mobile experience and transitions are prototyped for better sensing.

3 — Development

The development phase is structured to allow time for testing and content population from client’s side. Websites are migrated to the server to be proved against all screen sizes and main browsers before launch.
